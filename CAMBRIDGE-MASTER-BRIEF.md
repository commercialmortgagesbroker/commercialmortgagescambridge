# City Market Brief — Commercial Property + Lending

## City: Cambridge, Cambridgeshire

Cambridge is the UK's most concentrated life-sciences and tech commercial market — Silicon Fen. Cambridge Biomedical Campus (CBC), Cambridge Science Park (CSP), Granta Park, Babraham Research Campus and Trinity-owned innovation parks anchor a deal flow dominated by **lab and office investment, owner-occupier life-sciences operators, and tight-supply central retail/leisure**. Vacancy is structurally low; rents and yields hold up firmer than the regional UK average. For commercial mortgages this means a market biased toward **investment refinance, owner-occupier life-sciences and professional-services freehold purchase, and a long-running flow of central HMO/semi-commercial activity** driven by the student and post-doc population. New build commercial supply is constrained but the planning pipeline is active — Greater Cambridge Shared Planning is processing meaningful change-of-use and major mixed-use.
